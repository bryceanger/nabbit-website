How it works

Nabbed. Sorted. Done.

Three steps. No double entry. No lost receipts. No month-end scramble.

1

Snap, forward, or drop

Photo on mobile, drag-and-drop on desktop, or forward email receipts to your unique Nabbit address. However you get receipts, Nabbit is ready.

2

Nabbit does the work

OCR reads every field. AI matches the merchant, assigns a category, and flags HST/GST automatically — no manual entry, no correction needed.

3

Sync and close

Expenses flow directly to QuickBooks Online. Your bookkeeper gets everything they need. Month-end closes itself.

Why Nabbit

Built for Canadian small business.
Nothing else.

One-tap capture

Snap a photo the moment you pay. Nabbit reads the merchant, amount, date, and HST automatically. No typing, no filing, no forgetting.

Smart categorisation

Receipts are filed into the right expense category before you open the app. Train it once and it learns your business for good.

CRA-compliant storage

Every receipt stored in full resolution for 7 years, encrypted at rest, and retrievable in seconds if CRA ever comes calling.

QuickBooks Online sync

Push expenses directly to QBO. No double-entry, no copy-paste errors. Your bookkeeper gets clean, categorised data automatically.

Multi-company portals

Manage receipts across multiple entities from a single login. Each company gets its own isolated portal — perfect for fractional CFOs.

HST auto-flagging

Every receipt is automatically checked for HST/GST. Missing tax info gets flagged before it becomes a problem at filing time.

About Nabbit

Built by accountants,
for accountants.

Nabbit was built out of frustration. After years of watching small business owners lose deductions, miss receipts, and spend their weekends reconciling paper stubs, we decided to fix it.

We're a Canadian company. Our product is built for Canadian tax rules — CRA compliance, HST/GST, QBO integration — not bolted on after the fact.

The name? "Nab it" — to seize suddenly. Like a rabbit snatching something up before it disappears. That's what Nabbit does to your receipts.
